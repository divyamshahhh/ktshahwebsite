Questions answered

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before you begin investing with us.

How much money do I need to start investing?
You can start a SIP with as little as ₹500 a month. There's no need for a large amount — consistency over time matters far more than the size of each instalment.
Do you charge me a fee for your advice?
As an AMFI-registered mutual fund distributor, we are compensated through commissions paid by the asset management companies (AMCs), as disclosed by regulation. You do not pay us a separate advisory fee for distribution services.
Is my money safe with mutual funds?
Mutual funds are regulated by SEBI and your investments are held by independent custodians — never by the distributor. Returns are market-linked and not guaranteed; we help you choose funds that match your risk profile.
How do I track my investments?
We have our own app, available on the App Store and Play Store. Once you're onboarded you'll get access — with real-time tracking of your portfolio, anytime.
Are my returns guaranteed?
No. Mutual fund returns are market-linked and never guaranteed. We help you choose funds suited to your goals and risk profile — disciplined, long-term investing is what smooths out the ups and downs.
Can I start, pause, or stop my SIP anytime?
Yes. SIPs are flexible — you can increase, pause, or stop them at any time without penalty (barring fund-specific lock-ins like ELSS). Staying consistent is what drives long-term results.
