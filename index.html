Questions answered

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before you begin investing with us.

How much money do I need to start investing?
You can start a Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) with as little as ₹500 per month. There's no need for a large amount — consistency over time matters far more than the size of each instalment.
Do you charge me a fee for your advice?
As an AMFI-registered mutual fund distributor, we are compensated through commissions paid by the asset management companies (AMCs), as disclosed by regulation. You do not pay us a separate advisory fee for distribution services.
Is my money safe with mutual funds?
Mutual funds are regulated by SEBI and your investments are held by independent custodians — never by the distributor. However, returns are market-linked and not guaranteed. We help you choose funds that match your risk profile, but mutual fund investments are subject to market risks.
How do I track my investments?
You'll receive consolidated statements and can access your portfolio digitally any time. We also schedule regular reviews to keep you informed and your plan on track.
Can I withdraw my money whenever I want?
Open-ended mutual funds offer high liquidity — you can redeem units on any business day. Some funds (like ELSS tax-savers) have a lock-in period, which we'll always explain clearly before you invest.
